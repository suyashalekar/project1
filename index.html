ABOUT OUR CEO

Mr. Mahendra Jagannath Yeole has completed his graduation (B.E.Civil) in the year 1989 from Bharathi Vidyapith College, Pune University. He started his business in September 1989. He constructed approx 20,00,000 sq.ft. area in Pune city in the period of 1989 to 2002. In that period, He constructed residential buildings, townships, and commercial projects. In September 1996 Mr. Mahendra Yeole started his own new Firm in the name of M/s. Parth Developers. M/S. PARTH DEVELOPER is Partnership firm having its Corporate Office at 1196/B. Shrusti Chember, Office no 9,3rd Floor, Ghole Road, Pune-411005. M/s. Parth Developers us Partnership of Mr. Mahendra Jagannath Yeole and Family.
